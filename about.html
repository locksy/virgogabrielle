virgogabrielle

she is an old soul

in a fast, new world

Northern Californian vocalist/producer/instrumentalist virgogabrielle takes listeners to the dreamland through her candid lyrics, R&B inspired guitar, and layered melodies. Her compositions are designed to make people feel something wavy,


something real, something genuine.

Listeners have described Virgo’s music on a spectrum

of soothing and dreamy to powerful and hard-hitting, bringing about a true duality. Veering between jazzy, even slightly edgy neo-soul and hip-hop, Virgo’s signature style is a fusion of many influences. Just to name a few, inspiration from Jessie Reyez, K.Flay, Dreezy and Nai Palm can be heard

Virgo’s music revolves around embracing the authentic self and being upfront about the good, the bad and everything in between. Through this unfiltered honesty, Virgo brings listeners to a place not easily found in a world full of hiding.

Virgo performs both solo and with her full band, particularly enjoying events that foster community through sharing music. Her EP release show held at Renaissance Square in June 2021 drew over 100 people, as herself and the band performed the entirety of her “999” project. Virgo has played around the United States with organizations such as Sofar Sounds, also taking her music to New York for the first time to perform at SOB’s via Major Stage in July 2022.
